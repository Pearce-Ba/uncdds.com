Our story

A sisterhood rooted in purpose and belonging.

We celebrate women who lead with confidence, care deeply for the communities around them, and believe in showing up for one another through every season of campus life.

From mentorship and philanthropy to campus leadership and celebration, our chapter is dedicated to building a home that is both elevated and grounded.

Scholarship

We support academic growth through study circles, peer mentorship, and shared accountability.

Service

We partner with local organizations and campus initiatives to make a visible impact.

Leadership

We cultivate confident voices across student government, philanthropy, and campus experiences.

Community

We create traditions, rituals, and friendships that stay with our members long after graduation.
